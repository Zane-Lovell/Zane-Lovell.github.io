Competed in case competition at Georgetown University based on HBS's "AQR's Momentum Funds (A)" case. Highlights from the presentation can be seen below. The full PowerPoint presentation can be found at (https://github.com/Zane-Lovell/Portfolio). Our ultimate recommendation for AQR was to continue support of the Large Cap Momentum fund, but to slowly start to withdraw from the Small Cap and International Momentum Funds. We arrived at this decision by utilizing regression analysis to calculate the alpha of these funds. We supported our conclusion by evaluating the revenue of AQR versus the total net assets of AQR.
